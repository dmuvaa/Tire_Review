Best Tires for Subaru Outback

Published on: August 22, 2023 Author: Dennis Muvaa

The Subaru Outback has always been a favorite among adventure enthusiasts and families alike. Its versatile capabilities on both urban streets and rugged terrains demand a tire that can keep up. With so many options available in the market, it can be a daunting task to pick the best fit. We've compiled a list of some of the top tires for your Subaru Outback, considering various factors such as performance, durability, and price.

Michelin Defender T+H

Michelin's Defender T+H is a standard touring all-season tire that promises longevity and fuel efficiency. Built with Michelin's MaxTouch Construction, this tire assures even wear, translating to more mileage for your buck. Furthermore, its IntelliSipe technology improves braking, especially on wet roads, ensuring safety in unpredictable conditions.
